Quick Bio

For press kits & conference programs

Dhrubajyoti Ghosh is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Data Science and Analytics at Kennesaw State University. He received his Ph.D. in Statistics from Washington University in St. Louis and completed a postdoctoral fellowship at Duke University. His research focuses on time series analysis, longitudinal clinical trials, and machine learning, with applications in biomedicine and social science. He has authored 20+ peer-reviewed publications in journals including Bernoulli, Statistics in Medicine, Journal of Multivariate Analysis, and IEEE Transactions on Computational Social Systems. He leads the Ghosh Lab at KSU, where he mentors graduate and undergraduate students in statistical research.
